Bullet Points:

  • Humane Mice Trap - No kill, just lock them in when they are trying to eat the peanut inside the trap. 32cm(12.3 inchs) lengthened catch cage make sure these little guys live comfortably inside until you free them.
  • Safety Rat Trap - Catch mouse without poison,glue and other dangerous mouse kill trap, you should immediately get this humane trap if you want to keep your children or pets out of those safety problem.
  • Smart & Durable Design - The pedal in mouse trap is supported by a highly sensitive spring, just a slight touch, it will trigger switch and close the door. Both the front and back doors are reinforced by metal sheets. Once the mice come in, it is very difficult to escape!
  • Catch and Release Simply - The perfect trap is just need open the door and add peanuts. Brown body and mouse-hole size door is powerful to reduce the mice's alertness. When you caught a mice, it's convenient to carry the trap and release mouse without touch it.

Product Description:

Humane Mouse Trap has endorsed by humane societies worldwide

Why choose Hyton?

  • 1. Hyton mouse trap is about 32cm, while others are only about 15cm, which means more living spaces for mice. Of course, it will not be like other mouse cage to pinch off the rat tail.
  • 2. Brown color and mouse-hole size door is easy to remove the vigilance of rats.
  • 3. You can get rid of the rat problem through a humane way instead of killing them.

How it works:

Place the bait: Find some peanut or hazelnut spread (choosing the right bait is important!), you just need open the front door, then add baits in.
Wait and catch mice: Place the mouse trap near walls, corners or someplace where there is evidence of the little mouse's presence (mouse dropping), keep the front door open.
Release them: When you caught a mouse, please take it out, and find some place where there is far away from your house (Otherwise they will visit you again!), and make sure there is plenty of food for them to survive.

Tips:

1. Choose the right bait, the rodents are primarily nut and seed eaters, so the mouse trap bait they are most strongly attracted to is peanut butter or hazelnut spread.
2. Mouse are very alert to new things, so they won't be easily trapped, especially big mouse, please be patient and waiting for them.
3. Change the bait frequently to keep it fresh and attractive.
4. 4g of the weight is enough to start the trap, but if you encounter a baby mouse, it may too light and it move so fast that cause the pressure becomes very small, please place a coin or anything else weighing less than 4g on the pedal (hold the door, and adjust the location of the coin to make sure the mouse trap will not be triggered immediately), it will provide extra pressure to get the trap work.
